# SWAPHANDS
A trusted student-to-student marketplace at VIT that lets peers easily buy, sell, or exchange books, gadgets, and everyday essentials within campus.

SwapHands: VIT Student Marketplace

SwapHands is a campus exclusive marketplace built for VIT students to seamlessly buy, sell, or exchange items within the university. From books and gadgets to hostel essentials and bicycles, students can list what they no longer need and connect with peers who do. Every user is verified with their VIT credentials, ensuring trust and safety across the platform.



The app makes trading simple with category-based listings, search filters, and quick chat options for negotiations. By encouraging reuse and exchange, SwapHands also promotes sustainability and reduces waste on campus. Students can even explore service exchanges like tutoring or project support, fostering collaboration beyond just products.



SwapHands empowers students to save money, declutter, and find what they need right inside their own community. It’s a trusted, student-driven economy designed to make campus life smarter and more connected.

Problem :

In VIT (or any large campus), students have things they want to sell or trade: books, lab equipment, cycles, room stuff, gadgets, etc.

Current solutions are either too broad, full of spam, or lack campus-specific trust.

Students need a safe, exclusive marketplace just for them (Which is not a Whatsapp group).

Solution (Swaphands):

VIT Marketplace: A campus-only buy/sell platform for students.

Works like OLX but restricted to VIT students (verified with institute email or student ID).

Students can post items, negotiate, and meet on campus.

Could even include service exchange (e.g., tutoring, project help, renting stuff).

Key Features:

Student Authentication:

Login with VIT email (e.g., @vitstudent.ac.in) or VTOP integration.

Listings:

Upload images, add price, category (books, gadgets, cycles, hostel items).

Search & Filter:

By category, price, or hostel/block for convenience.

Chat/Negotiation:

In-app chat or redirect to WhatsApp.

Trust & Safety:

Only verified VIT users.

Option to report spam/fraud.



AI-based price suggestions (e.g., “A used Dell laptop usually sells for ₹20k–25k”).

Smart recommendations (suggesting items based on your interests).

A points system for trust (like sellers get ratings).

Optional payment gateway for secure transactions.

Gamification (badges for active sellers/buyers).



Tech Stack:

Frontend: React / Next.js.

Backend: Node.js + Express / Flask.

Database: Firebase (easy auth + storage) or MongoDB.

Auth: Google OAuth restricted to @vitstudent.ac.in.

Hosting: Vercel
